# Roles (Solo Adaptation)

This is a solo project; all Scrum roles are held by one developer but kept conceptually distinct
so each perspective is exercised honestly (SRS §7.2).

- **Product Owner** — maintains the product backlog, prioritizes user stories, defines acceptance
  criteria. Decisions are recorded in writing to preserve the perspective.
- **Scrum Master** — runs sprint ceremonies on a written schedule, tracks blockers on the board,
  enforces the Definition of Done.
- **Developer** — implements user stories, writes tests, maintains the codebase.
- **Code Owner** — self-reviews PRs against a checklist before merging; PRs stay open at least
  30 minutes to encourage genuine re-reading.

## Primary user

The developer tracking their own finances across Egyptian bank accounts (CIB, Banque Misr, NBE)
and cash. Key needs: speed of entry, accurate categorization, useful insights, and privacy
(local-first, no third-party financial aggregators).
